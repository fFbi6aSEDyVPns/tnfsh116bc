Class Events 活動

Our journey of international exchange and cultural learning

2021.09.28

與日本兵庫縣柏原高校線上交流

同學們透過電腦硬體與日本學生交流在地文化的特色

Our school and Kaibara High School established ties in 2020. Kaibara High School was the first Japanese school to contact our school to inquire about holding online meetings between students as a means of cultural exchange. 2021 marked the second year of our cooperation, and our meetings are carried out in three stages.

2021.09.29

與柏原高校視訊討論Glocal Summit發表主題

Kaibara High School invited our school to participate in the 2021 Glocal Summit held on December 22. Five students from our school (218 Li Yizhan, 217 Xu Zhaoting, 115 Xiaojing, Wang You, 117 Wang Boen) will participate in the presentation during this conference.

Students will be sharing their research results on their chosen topics during the summit.

2021.10.20

水校暨豐詰班與日本一宮西高校音文班線上交流

一宮西高校與我們的學校是好夥伴，特別致歉日本文害家森喜朗對無語症高中

We have been close friends with Ichinomiya Nishi High School in Aichi Prefecture, Japan. Since our first meeting in 2016, we have kept the exchange program going in spite of the pandemic. This year, we established our first-ever bilingual program, whose students had an online meeting.

2021.11.16

本校與美國德州達拉斯市卡羅爾高中一台美雙語視訊會議

Our school successfully established ties with Carroll High School in Dallas, Texas, USA through the assistance of Ms. Yang, Head of the education division of the Taipei Economic and Cultural Representative Office in Houston. We are looking forward to carrying out student exchange activities for the two schools in the future.

On 11/16, principals and teachers from both schools participated in the meeting.

2021.12.03

本校學生視訊會議 School視訊活動

Students from our school participated in an open pal project. Students from Carroll High School shared their first-ever interaction with our students from TNFSH.

After a couple months of being exchanged by March this year, an online student-to-student conference, with students being arranged for both each other between the schools.
